What's inside

Everything a serious
athlete needs

Built feature-by-feature for physique athletes — not casual step counters. Every tool is designed around the way bodybuilders actually train and eat.

🥩
Macro Tracking

Log food with USDA FoodData Central search, Open Food Facts fallback, barcode scanning, AI food photo recognition, voice entry, and a full recipe builder.

🏋️
Workout Logging

Built-in program library, fully custom program creator, auto-progression tracking, rest timers, and complete workout history.

📸
AI Food Scanner

Point your camera at any meal and get instant macro estimates. Powered by your own API key — no subscription required.

🧠
AI Coach

Weekly training and nutrition suggestions generated from your actual logged data. Real context, not generic advice.

👤
Coach Mode

Share your data with a real coach via Google Drive. Coaches can push macro targets and training programs directly into your app.

📊
Progress Tracking

Body measurements, progress photos with pose tagging, time-lapse comparisons, and trend charts across every metric that matters.

☁️
Google Drive Sync

All your data syncs to your personal Google Drive. Switch devices seamlessly. NARBE LLC never touches it.

📱
Install Anywhere

APE is a PWA — install it from your browser on any phone, tablet, or desktop. No app store. Works fully offline.
